## Envelopes

1. Percussion Envelope
fast attack
fast decay
zero sustain

Instant retro percussion.

2. Pad Envelope
slow attack
high sustain
slow release

Ambient swelling texture.

3. Pluck
instant attack
medium decay
low sustain
short release

Classic synth pluck.

4. Pitch Envelope

Huge educational value.

You will suddenly understand:

kick drums,
zaps,
retro arcade sounds.
5. Envelope-Controlled Fold

Very expressive.

Attack becomes:

harmonically explosive,
then settles.
